Governance in the Trenches
Where Governance Meets Resilience
Corporate governance is often reduced to policies, compliance checklists and boardroom formalities. At Anchorpoint Resilience Solutions, we take a different approach: governance is a resilience system—a framework that enables organizations to anticipate shocks, withstand disruption and recover with integrity.
Led by Zachary Kabera, PhD Candidate in Business Administration (Leadership & Corporate Governance) and author of Unshaken and Cracking the Code, our governance programs combine academic rigour, practical experience and real-world insight from audit firms, NGOs and private business ventures.
Why “Governance in the Trenches”?
Zachary’s professional journey has taken him through environments where:
* Projects were abruptly shut down
* Partnerships collapsed under internal conflict
* Organizations struggled under resource constraints
* Leaders faced ethical dilemmas with no easy answers
* Decisions affected livelihoods, trust and institutional stability
These experiences shaped Governance in the Trenches: Overcoming Business Failures with Resilience, a framework that addresses the realities leaders face in volatile and resource-constrained contexts.
Our Governance Philosophy
Just as climbers rely on preparation, discipline and ethical judgment to reach the summit, organizations survive and thrive when leadership, accountability and adaptive systems are in place.
Our philosophy emphasizes:
* Clear roles and accountability
* Ethical decision-making under pressure
* Risk awareness and disciplined planning
* Adaptive capacity in changing environments
* Human-centered governance that protects people, not just structures
Poor governance on a mountain can cost lives. Poor governance in an organization can cost futures. We help leaders build systems that are practical, resilient and sustainable.
